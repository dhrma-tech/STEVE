# Counselling Platform for JEE Students — Business Plan

## What This Business Does
This platform provides a comprehensive counselling service tailored to high school students preparing for the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE). It offers academic strategy and study tips, test-taking strategies and practice exams, career guidance for engineering admissions, mental health support during stressful times, additional resources like past test papers, video lectures, and study materials, a unique gamification feature, personalized mentorship and tutoring sessions, live Q&A sessions with top JEE educators, and a community forum for peer support and collaboration.

## The Problem
High school students preparing for the JEE face numerous challenges such as academic pressure, test anxiety, lack of resources, insufficient guidance, and limited peer support. These issues hinder their performance and overall well-being during this critical phase of their life.

## The Solution
Our platform addresses these problems by offering personalized counselling, tutoring, and resources to help students excel in their JEE preparation. It focuses on holistic development, ensuring that students not only improve academically but also build essential skills, receive career guidance, and receive mental health support when needed.

## Who Will Use It
Our ideal customer is a high school student preparing for the JEE (Recommended). They are likely to be aged between 16-19 years old, have a strong academic record, and are eager to secure admission into top engineering colleges in India. They may struggle with time management, test anxiety, or lack of resources and support, making our platform an essential tool for their success.

## How It Makes Money
We will employ a subscription-based model: Students pay a monthly/yearly fee for access to all services on the platform (Recommended). Offering multiple pricing tiers based on the level of service or resources could cater to different student needs and budgets.

## Your Edge Over Competitors
Our platform offers personalized mentorship and tutoring sessions, provides additional resources like past test papers, video lectures, and study materials not available elsewhere, focuses on holistic development of students including mental health support, career guidance, and skill building alongside academic help, and offers a unique gamification feature to make learning more engaging (Recommended).

## First 90 Days — Getting Customers
1. **Build MVP**: Launch a minimum viable product with the core features like personalized study plans, mental health support, and access to resources.
2. **Market Research**: Gather feedback from potential customers to validate our solution and identify areas for improvement.
3. **Partnerships**: Collaborate with schools, tutoring centers, and online learning platforms to reach more students.
4. **Content Creation**: Create engaging content such as video lectures, study materials, and practice exams to attract and retain users.
5. **Onboarding Campaign**: Run targeted marketing campaigns to onboard the first paying users and gather testimonials for future marketing efforts.

## Numbers That Matter
1. Number of registered users
2. Monthly active users (MAUs)
3. Churn rate
4. Revenue per user (RPU)
5. Conversion rate from free trial to paid subscription
6. Number of hours spent on the platform by users
7. Student success stories and testimonials

## Risks and How to Handle Them
1. **Competition**: To combat competition, focus on offering unique features and personalized services that cater specifically to JEE students.
2. **Acquisition Costs**: By partnering with schools, tutoring centers, and online learning platforms, we can reduce customer acquisition costs while reaching a larger audience.
3. **Retention**: Offer incentives such as discounts or additional resources for continuous use of the platform to increase retention rates.
